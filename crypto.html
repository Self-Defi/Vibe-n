Crypto Pay

Pay the invoice, save the tx hash, then verify on Wallet Connect.

  1. Open the crypto invoice link (Coinbase Commerce or your rail).
  2. Select the correct asset (USDC / ETH) required by the invoice.
  3. Confirm network exactly matches the invoice network.
  4. Send payment → save tx hash.
  5. Go to Wallet Connect → submit tx hash → unlock.
