Buy & Send (Step-by-step)
Beginner-safe
Buy crypto with fiat
  1. Open your on-ramp (Stripe / Coinbase / other).
  2. Buy USDC (recommended) on the correct network (Base / Ethereum / Polygon).
  3. Confirm you can see the balance in your wallet/app.
Tip: USDC reduces “oops” risk vs volatile coins.
Send crypto to the payment address
  1. Copy the payment address from the checkout screen.
  2. Verify the network matches (don’t mix chains).
  3. Send the exact amount shown.
  4. Wait for confirmations → you’ll be marked paid.
Rule: Address + network must match, every time.
Common mistake to avoid
Sending on the wrong chain. If checkout says Base, don’t send on Ethereum.
