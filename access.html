Wallet Connect

After you pay, you’ll verify purchase, then unlock access.

  1. Complete Fiat or Crypto payment.
  2. Save proof (receipt ID for fiat, tx hash for crypto).
  3. Connect wallet (this is your gate button on your platform/app).
  4. Verify proof (receipt / tx hash) → access is granted.
