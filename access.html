Wallet Connect

After you pay, connect your wallet and confirm proof (receipt / tx hash). This is the access gate.

  1. Complete payment (Fiat or Crypto).
  2. Save your proof: receipt (fiat) or tx hash (crypto).
  3. Tap your connect button (WalletConnect / on-site gate) and verify.
